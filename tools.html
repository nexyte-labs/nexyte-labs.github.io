Attendance Tracker

FREE

A smart attendance tracking tool built for students to monitor their subject-wise attendance and stay eligible for exams. It helps students calculate how many classes they need to attend to maintain the minimum 75% attendance — or how many classes they can safely miss if they are already above the limit.

  • ✔ Subject-wise attendance tracking
  • ✔ Calculates required classes to reach 75%
  • ✔ Shows how many classes you can miss if attendance is above 75%
  • ✔ Simple, student-friendly interface
  • ✔ Lightweight, fast, and easy to use

Ideal for college and university students following the 75% attendance rule.
